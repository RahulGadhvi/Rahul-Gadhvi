About Me

Hello! I'm Rahul Gadhvi, a passionate Technical Assistant at the Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar, deeply immersed in the exciting world of **AI Research and Natural Language Processing (NLP)**. My work primarily focuses on the intricate challenges of NLP, particularly in **Code-Mixed Language Processing**, where I strive to develop innovative solutions.

With a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from Gujarat Technological University, I bring a solid academic foundation combined with practical experience. I've had the privilege of contributing to cutting-edge projects with the **Lingo Labs team**, including a recent hands-on workshop on "AI for Libraries" for professionals across India.

Beyond research, I also have experience as a WordPress Developer Intern, enhancing my skills in web development and SEO. I am constantly learning, exploring new technologies, and always eager to take on challenges that push the boundaries of AI and its applications. I am actively **open to new opportunities** that allow me to contribute to impactful projects and further my growth in the AI domain.
